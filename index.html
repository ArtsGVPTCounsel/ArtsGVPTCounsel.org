    Summary of Findings: Systemic Dereliction of Duty

The assertion that there have been zero new certificated arts teachers hired by key reporting districts for FY 2023–24 is supported by official data indicating non-expenditure and programmatic delay, which collectively constitutes a systemic failure to meet the core legal objective of the Act.1
